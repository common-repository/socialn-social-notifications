== Description ==

SocialN is a service that helps you promote your social media accounts in your website. It shows your social media posts to your visitors as inline notifications.

In order to use this plugin, you should [sign up](https://socialn.io/?signup) to [SocialN](https://socialn.io). After signing up, you should create a website and connect your social media accounts to [SocialN](https://socialn.io).

== Installation ==

1. Upload `SocialN` plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate `SocialN` plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to `Settings`, `SocialN` page and enter your `API key` which you obtained from [SocialN Panel](https://socialn.io/manage)
